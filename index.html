Jinja : Hidden gem of East Africa

Also known as the adventure capital of East Africa, Jinja is located in Uganda. Cosily nestled on the banks of Lake Victoria at the start of mighty White Nile. Come visit, there’s something for everyone…

A taste of what Jinja offers the avid explorer...


Kayak the Nile offers a range of activities from a relaxing flat water paddle to full day on grade 5 rapids in a tandem kayak!
It's a short drive to the unspoilt tropical Mabira Rainforest. Numerous trails suitable for beginners to experienced hikers alike.
Enjoy the tranquility of Lake Victoria with a gin & tonic, whilst a local guide shares their culture and tells you unbelievable folk tales.
All Terrain Adventures takes you off the beaten path on a four-wheel through villages and the most stunning scenery.
Head to the Jinja main market and find quality craftman-ship in crazy designs whilst supporting the local economy.
